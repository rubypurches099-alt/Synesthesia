◼ KANDINSKY MODE ◼

AESTHETIC: Inspired by Wassily Kandinsky's abstract expressionist works, this visualization transforms music into geometric forms with bold primary colors.

VISUAL STYLE: Each note becomes a unique geometric shape - circles, triangles, squares, and lines - arranged in dynamic compositions.

COLOR PALETTE: Pure primary colors (red, yellow, blue) with black outlines in the Bauhaus tradition. Each note has its assigned geometric form.

PHILOSOPHY: Kandinsky believed in the spiritual connection between color, form, and sound. This visualizer embodies his theory of synesthesia.
