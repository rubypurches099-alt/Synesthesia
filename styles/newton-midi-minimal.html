Minimal Philosophy

Aesthetic: This visualization strips away all color to reveal the pure essence of musical form. In the absence of chromatic distraction, shape, size, and movement become the sole carriers of meaning.

Visual Language: Each note manifests as a simple circle. Velocity determines size and opacity. Time determines position. Nothing more, nothing less.

Design Principle: Following the minimalist tradition of artists like Agnes Martin and Donald Judd, every element serves a purpose. There is no decoration, only information.

Experience: Watch music become architecture. Observe patterns emerge from simplicity. Find beauty in restraint.
